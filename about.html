About the project

This website aims to gather fursuit makers in one place so that anyone who wants to commission a fursuit can quickly and clearly see who they are and what work they do.

In short: a simple and visual directory for buyers and curious people to find makers who might otherwise remain hidden by using other networks.

How it works (quick overview)

  • Makers sign up by filling out a form.
  • I review the profile to prevent fake profiles or scams.
  • In the future we will add more safety tools.

If your profile is in order, it will appear in the search results.

Security and verification

Currently, verification is manual. Later, badges, reviews, and a "verified" system will be added to reduce fraud and increase trust.
